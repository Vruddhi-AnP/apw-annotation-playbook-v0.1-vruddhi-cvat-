How to fill:
Read this before starting annotation. Follow the rules exactly.

Project Name:
Toy_Object_Detection_Project

Label: Person
What to label:
Any visible human body (full or partial).

Do not label:
- Statues
- Drawings or posters
- Reflections in mirrors

How to draw:
Draw a tight rectangle around the visible body.

Label: Car
What to label:
Cars, vans, and jeeps.

Do not label:
- Bikes
- Trucks
- Toy cars

How to draw:
Rectangle should cover the full vehicle.

Label: Dog
What to label:
Any real dog visible in the image.

Do not label:
- Dog statues
- Cartoons
- Other animals

How to draw:
Rectangle around the dog body, even if partially visible.

Validation rules:
- Each object gets only one label
- Boxes should not be too loose or too tight

Example:
If an image has 1 person and 1 car, draw 2 boxes with correct labels.
